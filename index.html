Design Process I Use

Step 1

Narrow and Discover

Crucial point to identify needs and aspects of the project. We are going to clarify the goals, requirements and specs of our future work.

Step 2

Ideate and Wireframe

Through research and wireframing we are going to find the best solutions possible. After approval the project skeleton is going to be UI-wraped.

Step 3

Verify and Design

With your project wireframe ready it's time to bring some aesthetics into. Here we are about to highlight brand identity, values and make things beautiful.

Step 4

Deliver and Support

Being structured and designed, the project is now ready for delivery and development. And once we are live, the project enters the support stage.
